Summary of Features
Phase Tracking: The Theater now cycles from Idle to Writing (optional), Rehearsing (14 days), and finally Performing.
Progress: Turn advancement automatically pushes the production forward.
Collaboration: Players can use the "Join Roles" button to assign themselves as the Writer, Director, or Performers.
Resolution: When rehearsals end, a chat card notifies the group to make their DC 15 Charisma (Performance) checks.
Scaling Rewards: The UI automatically calculates the correct Theater Die size (d6, d8, or d10) based on the character's level.
